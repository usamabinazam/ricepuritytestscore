What is the Rice Purity Test?

The rice purity test is a 100-question self-assessment quiz that measures life experiences using simple yes or no answers. Originally introduced as a social tool, it is now widely used online for entertainment and self-reflection. The test helps users understand their level of experience through a structured scoring system.

How the Rice Purity Score Works

Your score begins at 100. Each “yes” answer reduces your score by one point. A higher score indicates fewer experiences, while a lower score reflects more exposure. The rice purity score is easy to understand and instantly calculated after completing the quiz.

What is a Good Purity Score?

There is no fixed definition of a good score. Most users fall between 60 and 85. Scores above 90 are considered high, while scores below 50 indicate more life experience. The interpretation depends on your personal journey.

Average Rice Purity Score by Age

Younger individuals often score higher, typically between 85 and 100. College-aged users usually range between 60 and 85, while adults may fall between 40 and 70. These ranges reflect natural life progression.

Why People Take the Test

The rice purity test is popular because it is simple, engaging, and shareable. People take it for curiosity, comparison, and entertainment. It is often used as a conversation starter among friends.

Is the Rice Purity Test Accurate?

This test is not scientific. It is designed for fun and reflection. While it provides insights into personal experiences, it should not be treated as a strict evaluation.
